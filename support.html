How do I sync my data across devices?

Solven uses iCloud to sync your data. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices and have iCloud enabled in your device settings.

Can I customize the timer intervals?

Yes! You can set custom work and break intervals in the app settings. The default is 25 minutes work, 5 minutes break (Pomodoro technique).

How do I change the audio environment?

Tap the audio button on the timer screen to open the audio selection menu. Choose from various environments like rain, forest, ocean, or cafe.

Why isn't my timer working in the background?

Make sure you've granted the necessary permissions in Settings > Solven > Background App Refresh. The app needs background access to continue timers.

How do I reset my statistics?

Go to Settings > Statistics > Reset Data. This will clear all your session history and statistics.

Can I use Solven without an internet connection?

Yes! Solven works offline. However, iCloud sync requires an internet connection to sync data across devices.

üêõ Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Update to the latest version of Solven
  4. If the problem persists, contact support with details

Audio not playing

  1. Check your device's volume settings
  2. Make sure your device isn't in silent mode
  3. Try selecting a different audio environment
  4. Check if other apps can play audio

Notifications not working

  1. Go to Settings > Solven > Notifications
  2. Make sure notifications are enabled
  3. Check your device's notification settings
  4. Ensure Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications

Data not syncing

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Verify you're signed in with the same Apple ID
  3. Check iCloud settings for Solven
  4. Try signing out and back into iCloud
